Strawberry smoothie bowl

ingredients
  • 1/4 cup of milk
  • 6 frozen strawberries
  • 1/2 frozen banana
  • 1 tsp chai seeds
  • 1 tb peanut butter
  • 1 scoop protien powerder
  • 1/4 cup ice
directions
  1. put everything in blender
  2. shake
  3. blend and shake every 5 seconds
  4. repeat until mixed
