bulking crepe

ingredients
  • 1/2 cup of eggwhites
  • 3/4 scoop protien powder
  • 1tbsp chocolate stuff?
  • 1/2 cup of sliced strawberries
  • 2 tbsp mini marshmellows
directions
  1. Mix together your egg whites, protein powder
  2. Allow batter to sit
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat
  4. Pour 1/4 - 1/3 cup of batter into pan and tip until bottom of pan is coated
  5. Cook approx. 1-1.5 minutes and flip
  6. Remove from pan and repeat until all batter is gone (these are crepes, so they should be thin)
  7. ill the center of each crepe with some sliced strawberries and mini marshmallows and roll up
  8. Drizzle with chocolate almond butter
