M and M smoothie bowl

ingredients
  • 1/4 cup of milk (maybe almond)
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 tsp chia seeds (optional)
  • 1 tb pb
  • 1 tb coco powder
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 scoop protien powder
  • 3/4 cup ice
  • couple m and ms
directions
  1. put everything in blender
  2. shake
  3. blend and shake every 5 seconds
  4. repeat until mixed
  5. top it off with a few more m and ms
